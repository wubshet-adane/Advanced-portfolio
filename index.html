Online Food Ordering & Delivery Platform

I designed an Online Food Ordering System that not only enables customers to browse menus, place orders, make payments, and track deliveries in real time, but also creates job opportunities for delivery workers, who can register, accept available delivery tasks, and earn per completed order. The platform supports a virtual hotel (cloud kitchen) model, allowing multiple restaurants or digital-only brands to operate under one system by managing menus, orders, and promotions without requiring physical dining spaces. With role-based access for customers, restaurants, deliveries, and admins, the system ensures efficiency, scalability, and a modern digital food ecosystem.
